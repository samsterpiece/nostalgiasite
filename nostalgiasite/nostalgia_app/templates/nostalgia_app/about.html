About Time Capsule

Time Capsule is an educational website designed to help people understand how the world has changed since their graduation. By entering your graduation year, you can explore historical events, outdated facts, and recommendations for further learning.

How it works

  1. Enter your graduation year on the home page.
  2. Browse through a timeline of events that have occurred since your graduation.
  3. Discover which facts or laws may have become outdated since you graduated.
  4. Get recommendations for books, documentaries, and other resources to learn more about recent developments.
  5. Save interesting events to your profile for later reference.

Our Mission

At Time Capsule, we believe that understanding how the world has changed is crucial for personal growth and staying informed. Our goal is to make this learning process engaging, accessible, and fun for everyone.
